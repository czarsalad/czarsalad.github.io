My current project

May 3, 2017 by Ethan

My current project is a small form factor watercooled PC, with the goal being a quiet, powerful computer with a subtle aesthetic look to it. A lot of people recommend using a larger form factor case for a first time watercooled build, but that means I'll eventually have to rebuild the computer eventually to get it the way I want it to. So, ignoring the recommendations of others, I have started my build in the Fractal Design Nano S case with a full size graphics card and two radiators.
