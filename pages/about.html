A bit about me...

Want to learn more about this site or about me as a human being? Just look below.

A boy and his computers.

Hello, my name is Ethan Campbell. I'm a 20 year old Web Development student at Mesa College and, in my free time, I build computers! A few years ago I built my first computer so that I could finally do the things I wanted to do (games, photo editing, etc) without being limited by the hardware I currently had, and since then I've ended up building three. I plan on using this site to keep track of all of the parts and progress I make in my current and future builds.

That's neat, but what about yourself?

Well, aside from being a student, I also work part time at a theater as a projectionist. I've gotten very familiar with running movies, both film and digital, as well as the day to day tasks and weekly requirements to make playlists. When I'm not playing games, doing school work, or running around at work I enjoy going on trips to Julian or riding my bike all anywhere from DownTown to Torrey Pines Beach.
